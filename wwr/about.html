About us

Welcome to the thrilling world of Adventure Rafting, where the spirit of adventure meets the rush of the river! Let's embark on a journey through the history of our rafting business.

Adventure Rafting is not just a business, it's a community, a family, and a celebration of the untamed beauty that rivers offer. Join us as we continue to ride the rapids, explore uncharted waters, and create memories that last a lifetime.

History

In the summer of 2008, a group of friends, bound by a shared love for the great outdoors, dared to dream. Founded by outdoor enthusiasts Sarah Mitchell and Jake Reynolds, Adventure Rafting started as a humble endeavor on the banks of the picturesque Rapids Creek. Our founders, driven by a passion for rafting, aimed to create an experience that would not only satiate the thirst for adventure but also foster a sense of camaraderie among nature lovers.

As word spread about the exhilarating experiences offered by Adventure Rafting, so did the river of enthusiasts seeking the thrill of the rapids. Our business grew, evolving from a small rafting operation to a comprehensive adventure hub. We expanded our fleet, diversified our trips, and developed a team of seasoned guides who share our commitment to safety, fun, and the preservation of our natural surroundings.
