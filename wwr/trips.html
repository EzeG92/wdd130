Trips

Jungle Expedition

Embark on a multi-day rafting expedition through a remote wilderness area. Participants will navigate challenging rapids, camp along the riverbanks, and immerse themselves in the pristine natural surroundings. This adventure offers a true escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

Moonlight Adventure

Experience the magic of rafting under the moonlight! This unique adventure takes place in the evening, providing a different perspective on the river and its surroundings. Participants will navigate the river with the soft glow of the moon, creating a serene and unforgettable experience.

MORE INFORMATION

Family Rafting

Tailored for families with younger adventurers, this rafting trip offers a mix of excitement and safety. Guides will lead families through milder rapids suitable for all skill levels, ensuring a fun and memorable day on the river. Snacks, games, and water activities for kids can also be included.

MORE INFORMATION

Extreme Rapids Challenge

For adrenaline junkies seeking the ultimate thrill, the Extreme Rapids Challenge is designed to push the limits. This excursion takes participants through the most intense rapids, offering a heart-pounding experience for those who crave excitement and want to test their rafting skills to the max.
