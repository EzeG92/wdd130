Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of the website is to serve as a comprehensive online hub that showcase the exciting adventures and recreational opportunities we offer. Through engaging multimedia content, such as captivating images and videos of rafting excursions, the website aims to inspire potential clients and convey the thrill of navigating the river's challenging rapids. In addition to acting as a virtual gateway, the website serves practical functions by providing essential information about the rafting packages on offer.

Audience

Who are the target customers? This website is destined to adventure and fitness enthusiasts, nature lovers, travelers and tourist, and everyone who loves outdoor sports and exciting adventures. What do they want? Have exciting experiences that connect them with nature where they can get out of their daily activities, clearing their minds, sharing pleasant moments with their friends and forging new friendships. How will the audience usually access to the site? Much of the target audience will access the site through mobile devices, especially those tourists who want to quickly find excursion options available nearby.
